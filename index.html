Frequently Asked Questions Maswali Yanayoulizwa Mara Kwa Mara

Is SwahiliRewards free to join? / Je, SwahiliRewards ni bure kujiunga?
Yes! Creating an account is completely free. However, to access all earning features (games, tasks, withdrawals), you need an active Premium subscription at TZS 9,500/month.
Ndiyo! Kufungua akaunti ni bure kabisa. Hata hivyo, ili kufikia vipengele vyote vya kupata pesa, unahitaji usajili wa Premium kwa TZS 9,500/mwezi.
How long does subscription verification take? / Uthibitisho wa usajili unachukua muda gani?
Subscription payments are manually verified by the admin team. This typically takes a few hours, though it may occasionally take up to 24 hours during busy periods. You'll receive a notification once activated.
Malipo ya usajili yanathiriwa kwa mkono na timu ya msimamizi. Hii kawaida huchukua masaa machache. Utapokea arifa ukiwa umewezeshwa.
Why is the Earn tab locked? / Kwa nini kichupo cha Mapato kimefungwa?
The Earn tab requires an active Premium subscription. If your subscription has expired or hasn't been verified yet, you'll see the lock screen. Tap "Subscribe Now" to activate or renew.
Kichupo cha Mapato kinahitaji usajili wa Premium unaofanya kazi. Ikiwa usajili wako umekwisha au haujathibitishwa bado, utaona skrini ya kufunga.
When do I receive my withdrawal? / Nitapokea uondoaji wangu lini?
Withdrawal requests are processed manually by the admin. Most are approved within 24 hours. You can track the status (Pending / Approved / Rejected) in your Withdrawal History on the Withdraw tab.
Maombi ya uondoaji yanashughulikiwa kwa mkono na msimamizi. Mengi yanaidhinishwa ndani ya masaa 24. Unaweza kufuatilia hali katika Historia ya Uondoaji.
Can I play all three games on the same day? / Ninaweza kucheza michezo yote mitatu siku moja?
Yes! All three games (Spin, Scratch Card, Lucky Number Draw) can be played daily. However, you can only play one at a time — the others lock while one is active. Complete or close one to unlock the next.
Ndiyo! Michezo yote mitatu inaweza kuchezwa kila siku. Hata hivyo, unaweza kucheza mmoja tu kwa wakati mmoja — wengine wanafungwa wakati mmoja ukifanya kazi.
How do I cancel my subscription? / Ninawezaje kufuta usajili wangu?
SwahiliRewards uses a manual payment model. There is no auto-renewal. To cancel, simply don't pay for the next month. Your subscription will expire on its end date, and access will be removed automatically.
SwahiliRewards inatumia mfumo wa malipo ya mkono. Hakuna kuhuisha moja kwa moja. Kutofuta, tu usilipe mwezi ujao. Usajili wako utaisha na ufikiaji utaondolewa.
What is the daily earning limit? / Kikomo cha mapato cha kila siku ni nini?
The platform has a daily earning cap to maintain sustainability. Once you hit it, a banner appears saying "Daily earning limit reached — come back tomorrow!" The limit resets at midnight. Check the Earn tab to see your remaining capacity.
Jukwaa lina ukomo wa mapato wa kila siku ili kudumisha uendelevu. Ukifika, ujumbe unaonekana. Kikomo kinawaka upya usiku wa manane.
My referral friend signed up — where is my reward? / Rafiki wangu wa rufaa alisajili — tuzo yangu iko wapi?
Your 2,000 TZS referral reward is credited once your referred friend subscribes and is verified as Premium. If they only registered but haven't subscribed yet, the reward hasn't been triggered. You can track it in the Referral History section.
Tuzo yako ya TZS 2,000 inaongezwa mara tu rafiki wako aliyeorodheshwa akijiunga na kuthibitishwa kama Premium. Unaweza kuifuatilia katika sehemu ya Historia ya Rufaa.
Is SwahiliRewards available outside East Africa? / Je, SwahiliRewards inapatikana nje ya Afrika Mashariki?
SwahiliRewards is designed primarily for users in Tanzania 🇹🇿, Kenya 🇰🇪, and Uganda 🇺🇬. Payment methods and currency options reflect these regions. If you are outside these countries, some features (especially withdrawals) may not work optimally.
SwahiliRewards imeundwa hasa kwa watumiaji wa Tanzania, Kenya, na Uganda. Njia za malipo na chaguzi za sarafu zinaonyesha maeneo haya.
